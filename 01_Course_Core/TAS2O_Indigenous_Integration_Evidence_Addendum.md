# TAS2O Indigenous Integration Evidence Addendum

This addendum documents where Indigenous perspectives and learning prompts are embedded across the course.

Minimum integration points:

- Unit 1: design thinking, cultural context, and respectful use of community references.
- Unit 2: product research, sustainability, and comparison of design choices across contexts.
- Unit 3: prototyping, feedback, and reflection on the social impact of design decisions.

Evidence to retain:

- Lesson plan references showing where Indigenous content appears.
- Project prompts that require students to consider community impact, stewardship, and responsible design.
- Rubrics that assess reflection, context awareness, and respectful research practice.

Filing guidance:

- Keep this addendum with the course outline and unit plans.
- Update the unit references if the project sequence changes.
- Do not limit Indigenous content to a single lesson or a one-time mention.
