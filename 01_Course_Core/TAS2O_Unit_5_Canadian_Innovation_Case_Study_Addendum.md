# TAS2O Unit 5 Canadian Innovation Case Study Addendum

This addendum adds a Canadian innovation case study to Unit 5.

Case study focus:

- Canadian non-Indigenous inventor, engineer, or technologist
- Emphasis on women and underrepresented groups where relevant
- Connection to manufacturing, product development, sustainability, or skilled trades

Suggested discussion prompts:

- What problem was solved?
- What practical design choices were made?
- What workplace, social, or community impact followed?
- How does the case study connect to current skilled trades pathways in Ontario?

Filing guidance:

- Attach this addendum to the Unit 5 plan and career pathway report materials.
- Use one or more short case-study paragraphs rather than a single sentence mention.
