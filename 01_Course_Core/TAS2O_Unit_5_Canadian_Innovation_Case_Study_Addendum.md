# TAS2O Unit 5 Canadian Innovation Case Study Addendum

This addendum adds a Canadian innovation case study to Unit 5 and provides a direct evidence bridge for Strand B expectations.

Case study focus:

- Canadian non-Indigenous inventor, engineer, or technologist
- Emphasis on women and underrepresented groups where relevant
- Connection to manufacturing, product development, sustainability, or skilled trades
- Clear linkage to user needs, community impact, and practical problem solving
- Discussion of how innovation choices were shaped by legal, ethical, social, economic, or environmental factors
- Reference to how Indigenous knowledge systems or community knowledge systems may inform problem solving where relevant

Suggested discussion prompts:

- What problem was solved?
- What practical design choices were made?
- What workplace, social, or community impact followed?
- How does the case study connect to current skilled trades pathways in Ontario?
- How do the case details show the relationship between user needs and technological solutions?
- Which factors influenced the innovation process?
- What transferable skills or career pathways are visible in the case?

- Use one or more short case-study paragraphs rather than a single sentence mention.
