# TAS2O Online and Blended Practical Work Guide

This guide identifies where practical learning occurs and how it is supervised.

Delivery settings:

- Design research, drafting, reflection, and digital planning may occur online.
- 3D printing and circuit-board work are completed on site unless the school has a separate approved supervised arrangement.
- Any home-based work is limited to non-hazardous planning, documentation, or reflection tasks.

Required evidence:

- Location of the practical work
- Supervision arrangement
- Device or platform used
- Safety control, if the activity involves equipment
- Follow-up record showing what was completed on site and what was completed remotely

Filing guidance:

- Link this guide to the online/blended learning evidence log.
- Do not imply that hazardous equipment is used unsupervised at home.
- Keep the blended learning record consistent with the lab setup checklist and safety package.
