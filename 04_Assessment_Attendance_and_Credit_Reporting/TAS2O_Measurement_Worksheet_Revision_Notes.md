# TAS2O Measurement Worksheet Revision Notes

This note records the requested measurement worksheet changes for Unit 2.

Required additions:

- A column for metric units
- A column for imperial units
- A column for conversion method
- A column for measured value or tolerance
- A column for comments on accuracy or quality control

Purpose:

- Make the measurement record explicit enough for verification during inspection.
- Show the connection between raw measurement data, unit conversion, and quality control.
- Support rubric language that distinguishes quantitative evidence from qualitative interpretation.

Filing guidance:

- Keep the revised worksheet with the Measurement and Quality Control Report materials.
- Update the teacher exemplar and rubric language to match the worksheet.
