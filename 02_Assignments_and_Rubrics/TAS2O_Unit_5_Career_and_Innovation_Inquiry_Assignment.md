# TAS2O Unit 5 Career and Innovation Inquiry Assignment

This assignment gives Unit 5 a clear, assessable task for Strand B and A1.7 evidence.

Student task:

- Research one Canadian innovation case and one additional supporting source about a technology field, skilled trade, or pathway.
- Include at least one community perspective source when available, such as a local expert, family member, community member, or published source from a First Nations, Metis, Inuit, or other diverse community perspective.
- Compare at least two pathways into the field, such as apprenticeship, college, university, workplace training, or in-school job-skills programs.
- Explain how sustainability, labour-market needs, technology change, or AI may affect the pathway.
- Reflect on the transferable skills the student is building and how those skills connect to future learning or work.

Required evidence:

- Research notes
- Source list
- Comparison chart or pathway map
- Written reflection or presentation notes
- One short paragraph linking the work to community, cultural, or Indigenous perspectives where relevant

Assessment links:

- Strand B: B1.1, B1.2, B1.3, B1.4, B2.1, B2.2, B2.3, B3.1, B3.2, B3.3, B3.4
- Strand A: A1.7
