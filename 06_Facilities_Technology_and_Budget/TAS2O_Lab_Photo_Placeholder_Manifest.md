# TAS2O Lab Photo Placeholder Manifest

This manifest identifies where the final facility photos will be filed.

Placeholder slots:

- Lab wide overview with school nameplate
- 3D printer close-up
- Circuit board station close-up
- Safety station and storage area
- Technology access or work surface overview

Current status:

- Demo placeholders may be used only until real dated photographs are available.
- Replace all placeholder images with current photographs before formal inspection submission.
- Ensure each final image clearly shows the school environment and the equipment in use or staged for use.

Filing guidance:

- Keep the photo set with the facilities inventory, lab setup checklist, and maintenance log.
- Add date, location, and caption notes to each image once the real photographs are uploaded.
